The college has constituted a committee under the chairmanship of Principal and other faculty members as given below to redress the complaints, regarding issues, women harassment, Ante-caste discrimination


Objectives of the Grievance Redressal Cell :

Grievances submitted by the students are addressed systematically with active involvement and cooperation of the respective college/office related with the grievance, maintaining necessary confidentiality in handling process. Grievance Redressal cell of the college has emerged into an appropriate platform for providing support and encouragement to all the students and expresses their grievances freely and frankly. It is striving to ensure a responsive and accountable attitude, secular ambience among the authorities concerned.
